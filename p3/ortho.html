Ortho Dash

Ortho Clinical Diagnostics

The Ortho Dash is a project I've been working on as a part-time employee at Ortho Clinical Diagnosics since February. Ortho Clinical Diagnostics is a company that makes medical analyzers, large machines that go in medical labs that are used to process and test blood and other fluids. We are developing this dashboard to allow lab technicians to monitor the statuses of their analyzers from afar without needing to constantly walk around to see if there's a problem that needs to be resolved. We are also trying to use the dashboard as a way to consolidate the flow of information that's coming and simplify it for the user.
